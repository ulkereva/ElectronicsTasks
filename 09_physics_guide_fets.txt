Description:
Physics Guide on Field Effect Transistors (FETs) — a 7-slide visual guide covering
JFET & MOSFET characteristics, parameters, biasing, and key questions/exercises.

Topics covered:
1. What is a FET? — voltage-controlled device, high input impedance, low power consumption
2. Types of FETs — JFET (Junction Gate FET) and MOSFET (Metal-Oxide Semiconductor FET)
3. JFET characteristics — Shockley equation, I_DSS, pinch-off voltage V_P
4. MOSFET characteristics — operating regions (cut-off, triode/linear, saturation), threshold voltage
5. Important parameters — drain current at V_GS = 0, pinch-off voltage, threshold voltage, transconductance, output resistance, input resistance (very high)
6. Biasing configurations — JFET biasing (gate–source reverse-biased by R_S) and MOSFET biasing (voltage divider)
7. Questions & Exercises — JFET transfer characteristics, comparing JFET vs MOSFET, designing a biasing circuit for a MOSFET and calculating the operating point
